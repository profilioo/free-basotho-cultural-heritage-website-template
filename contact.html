Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to the cultural archive?

We welcome stories, photographs, traditional knowledge, and cultural artifacts. Contact us through the form above with "Share Cultural Stories" as your topic of interest.

Can I use your content for educational purposes?

Yes! Our content is available for educational use. Please contact us for proper attribution guidelines and any specific permissions you may need.

Do you organize cultural tours or workshops?

We partner with local cultural organizations to offer authentic experiences. Select "Collaboration Opportunities" in the contact form to learn more.

How can schools use this website?

Teachers can use our content for cultural education. We also offer educational resources and can arrange virtual presentations for classrooms.

Community Guidelines

Respectful Cultural Sharing

When sharing cultural information or stories, please ensure accuracy and respect for traditional knowledge. We encourage:

  • Authentic, firsthand experiences
  • Proper attribution of sources
  • Respectful representation of traditions
  • Cultural sensitivity in descriptions
  • Permission from community elders when appropriate

Basotho Heritage

Preserving and celebrating the rich cultural heritage of the Basotho people for future generations.
